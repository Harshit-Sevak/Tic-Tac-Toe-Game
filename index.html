Tic Tac Toe

(i) Two players: one uses "X," the other uses "O." (ii) The game is played on a 3x3 grid. (iii) Players take turns, placing their symbol in an empty square. (iv) A player wins by getting three of their symbols in: A horizontal row A vertical column A diagonal (v) If all 9 squares are filled without a winner, the game is a draw. (vi) A player cannot place a symbol in a square that's already occupied.
